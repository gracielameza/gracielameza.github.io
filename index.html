Gracie Talks About Anime

Since I can (I guess) write about anything, I'll just talk about an anime I like, and it's considered as a fairly normal beginner entry for people who don't usually like anime. Your Name is about two teenagers who wake up one day, only to discover that they have switched bodies. As they both try to figure out why this is happening, they learn more about each other and learn more about their connection. This is a romantic fantasy film, and it's beautifully animated.
